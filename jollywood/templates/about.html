Jollywood

Jollywood is a small tutoring school located in a suburban town in Tashkent and is close to the center (20 - 30 minutes away). Our little institution has been helping those who want to learn languages and achieve their goals for about two years.

Starting with only 2 teachers and a couple of small rooms, Jollywood managed to become recognized as one of the most effective teaching centers in Uzbekistan with its supportive team, flexible timetables and affordable prices.

Our Teachers

Jollywood has a great team of teachers who are always supportive and friendly with both teachers and students.

Currently, we have 5 teachers employed. Two of them are English teachers with high qualifications (Mr. Tokhir IELTS: 7.5, Mr. Salim IELTS: 7.5) and skills, while two others are Russian teachers with 8+ years of experience. And finally at the moment we have one Mathematics teacher, though it seems we'll have to hire more as demand for learning Math has increased in recent months.
